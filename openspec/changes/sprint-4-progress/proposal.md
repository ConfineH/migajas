# Proposal: Sprint 4 — Levels & Progress

## Intent

Give learning structure and a sense of advancement: levels 1–5, unlock rules, visible progress, mastery score, and retry of failed exercises.

## Capabilities

- `progress` — Level completion, mastery score, unlock rules (60% pass threshold)
- `levels-2-5` — Exercise content for cereales, legumbres, platos mixtos, casos combinados
- `retry` — Re-practice failed exercises per level

## Success Criteria

- Progress persists via HTTP cookie
- Level N+1 unlocks when level N is passed (≥60%)
- Progress page shows completions, mastery, and retry CTA
- Failed exercises can be retried
